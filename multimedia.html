A photograph I shot and editted myself for one of my college courses, multimedia reporting was published in the Independent Florida Alligator. The photograph features Gainesville city commissioner Susan Bottcher, Alachua County Emerging Leaders' president Andrew Romero and others. The photo assignment was one of three pieces, including a video and an audio interview piece, both of which I recorded and edited myself.

Next is an interview style video storytelling project I shot and editted myself. Featured in the video is Gainesville city commissioner Susan Bottcher and a University of Florida student who both weighed in on the new bike pump installations in downtown Gainesville.

The audio portion of this assignment, featured below, which I recorded using my iPhone and edited with Audacity, was actually the most difficult for me to produce. Having very little knowledge of audio work, I essentially taught myself how to cut and sew the audio together. I was also required to produce and record my own natural sound for the project, which, given the piece I chose to report on, was quite difficult.
