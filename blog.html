WHAT'S GOOD IN GAINESVILLE

What’s Good In Gainesville is a project un-derway in which I will scope out, discover and give a detailed account of those hidden gems within the Gator Nation. More specif-ically, those underappreciated boutiques, store, and quaint restaurants throughout Gainesville, Fla.

The blog is dedicated to helping promote these local businesses as well as give readers a heads-up as to what to expect to experience when frequenting these establishments. I hope to open the eyes of Florida Gators to the wonders that await them if they're brave enough to venture from campus.

WGIG started as an assignment for my multimedia writing class but has since gained momentum and become a side project of mine. While I have little to no interest in becoming a business critic, the blog gave me an outlet for my writing and an excuse to explore the city.
