WHAT'S GOOD IN GAINESVILLE

What’s Good In Gainesville is a project un-derway in which I will scope out, discover and give a detailed account of those hidden gems within the Gator Nation. More specif-ically, those underappreciated boutiques, stores, and quaint little restaurants throughout Gainesville, Florida.

The blog is dedicated to helping promote these local businesses as well as give readers a heads-up as to what to expect to experience when frequenting these establishments. I hope to open the eyes of Florida Gators to the wonders that await them if they're brave enough to venture from campus.

Many of my post include negativities aimed towards businesses. However, this is just my honest opinions and experiences. I show no bias when reviewing or critiquing these businesses and my reviews are based on my experiences and my experiences alone. No one else has a say in what I post, keeping it 100% genuine.

WGIG started as an assignment for my multimedia writing class but has since gained momentum and become a side project of mine. While I have little to no interest in becoming a business critic, the blog gave me an outlet for my writing and an excuse to explore the city. I often struggle to find something worth writing.

Microblogging, such as through mediums like Tumblr, Pinterest or Polyvore are fun, but do they mean anything? I feel as though What's Good In Gainesville could actually mean something.

I'm also currently working on a music blog in which myself and others rate, review and discuss music, new artists, albums and maybe a bit of fashion. I hope to have the project up and running by the end of summer 2014.
