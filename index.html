About

My name is Bradley Norman and I am a public relations student enrolled at the University of Florida. My interests include art, both fine and street, fashion, film and the entertainment industry as a whole, politics, and journalistic and fiction writing.

I hope to one day work as a public relations practitioner within the corporate sector. Despite my desire to pursue a career in the field of public relations, I truly enjoy writing.
